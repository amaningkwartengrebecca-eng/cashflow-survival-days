❓ FAQ
What is the "Adjusted Survival Days"?

It's how many days your business can survive if no new income arrives, after subtracting unpaid invoices and factoring in a possible client payment delay.

How is statutory interest calculated?

Under UK law, you can charge 8% plus the Bank of England base rate (currently 3.75%) on late payments from other businesses. That's 11.75% per year.

How do I add a recurring direct debit?

Click the "🏦 Add Direct Debit" button on the Invoices tab. Enter the name, amount, first due date, and contract length.

Can I import data from Google Sheets?

Yes. Publish your sheet as CSV, then click "📥 Import from Sheets" and paste the URL.

What happens when I run out of hearts in the quizzes?

You can't answer more questions until a heart regenerates. Hearts regenerate every 10 minutes.
